Artificial Intelligence:

is the ability of a computer program to learn and think. John McCarthy coined the term ‘Artificial Intelligence’ in the 1950s.

He said, ‘Every aspect of learning or anyother feature of intelligence can in principle be so precisely described that a machine

can be made to simulate it.An attempt will be made to find how to make machines use language, form abstractions, and concepts,

solve kinds of problems now reserved for humans, and improve themselves.
